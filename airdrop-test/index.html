How much will I get and how to claim?

5% of the DeFi Blockchain Foundation wallet (total 14.4 million DFI) has been pledge in the Airdrop. The amount of DFI you are eligible to claim is determined by a snapshot of your BTC hodlings at Bitcoin block 647,500 (estimated to be mined on 9 September, 2020 22:00 UTC).
